Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

KSP Plugins may process Discord identifiers, order and licence records, activation identifiers, support messages, attachments, and technical logs needed to operate the service.

Payment-card and banking information is handled by the checkout provider and is not stored by KSP Plugins.

Information is used for licensing, activation control, customer roles, downloads, updates, support, security, fraud prevention, and audit records.

Customers should redact passwords, tokens, credentials, and unrelated personal data before submitting logs.

For privacy requests, contact kspsupplugins@hotmail.com.
