Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

This policy assumes a static website with no analytics. Update it if you add analytics, advertising, account registration, or more data processors.

1. Information processed

When you purchase, activate a license, use the Discord bot, or request support, KSP Plugins may process Discord identifiers, order records, product and license records, activation identifiers, support messages, attachments, and technical security logs.

2. Payments

Payment-card and banking information is handled by the checkout or payment provider. KSP Plugins does not need to store complete card details.

3. Purposes

Information is used to provide purchasing, licensing, activation control, customer roles, downloads, updates, support, fraud prevention, security, and audit records.

4. Diagnostics

Customers should redact passwords, tokens, account credentials, personal messages, and unrelated personal data before submitting logs. Automated redaction can reduce risk but cannot guarantee every sensitive value is detected.

5. Sharing

Information may be processed by service providers required to operate the website, Discord bot, payment system, hosting, email, or licensing API. Personal information is not sold as an independent product.

6. Retention and rights

Records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for service delivery, legal obligations, disputes, fraud prevention, and legitimate operational records. Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability.

7. Security

Reasonable safeguards are used, including restricted administration, encrypted license storage, signature verification, and access-controlled support areas. No system can be guaranteed completely secure.

8. Contact

Privacy requests should be sent through the support email or private Discord support system shown on the main website.
