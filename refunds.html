Refund Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Refund requests for digital products are reviewed case-by-case. Mandatory consumer rights remain unaffected.

Contact support with the order reference, product name, purchase date, and reason. Eligibility may depend on applicable law, download and activation status, product use, the nature of the issue, and the payment provider's procedures.

Technical issues should first be reported with the plugin version, client version, reproduction steps, and a redacted log.

Contact kspsupplugins@hotmail.com or use the KSP Plugins Discord.
