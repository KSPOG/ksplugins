Refund Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Refund rights vary by country and by the delivery of digital content. Review this policy against the checkout provider's rules and applicable consumer law.

1. Digital products

KSP Plugins sells digital software licenses and related access. Delivery may occur immediately after successful payment.

2. Requests

Contact support with the order reference, product name, purchase date, and reason for the request. Do not post payment or identity information in public Discord channels.

3. Eligibility

Eligibility depends on applicable law, the checkout provider's procedures, the product terms, whether access or download has begun, and whether the license has been activated or substantially used.

4. Technical issues

Customers should first provide the plugin version, client version, reproduction steps, and a redacted log. A compatibility or configuration issue may be resolved through support or an update.

5. Abuse

Refunds may be refused where legally permitted when there is evidence of license sharing, redistribution, fraudulent payment activity, chargeback abuse, or tampering with access controls.

6. Subscriptions

Cancellation normally stops future renewal and does not automatically refund the current paid period. Final access dates are controlled by the checkout provider and product terms.

7. Statutory rights

Nothing in this policy removes consumer rights that cannot legally be waived. Applicable legal requirements take precedence.

8. Contact

Refund requests should be submitted through the support email or private Discord support system shown on the main website.
