FAQ

Before you purchase

Where are TwiLite plugins purchased?

Through the official TwiLite webstore. KSP Plugins provides documentation, updates, and Discord support.

How are Microbot licenses delivered?

After successful payment through the KSP storefront. License information is available privately through the Discord bot.

Are plugins guaranteed to remain compatible?

No. Client, game, Java, and API updates can require plugin changes. Compatibility is tracked per release.

Are automation plugins risk-free?

No. Customers are responsible for following the rules of each game, client, marketplace, and platform they use.

TRANSPARENCY
